## 🙋‍♂️ About Me

I’m Ivan Susendra (Vanszas), a game developer from Indonesia focused on Unreal Engine 5, gameplay systems, horror game mechanics, UI/UX implementation, and 3D asset creation. My goal is to build games that are visually appealing, optimized, creative, and enjoyable for everyone.

I’m also the Co-Founder of **Monokotil Studio**, an indie game team specializing in psychological and first-person horror experiences.

---

## 🛠️ Currently Building

I am currently developing **DontIn**, a first-person psychological horror game focused on atmosphere, interaction, cinematic horror flow, and technical gameplay systems in Unreal Engine 5.

---

## 💻 My Role

I work across game design, gameplay programming, UI/UX implementation, 3D asset creation, and technical systems development. My focus is not only designing features, but also implementing them directly into playable game systems.
